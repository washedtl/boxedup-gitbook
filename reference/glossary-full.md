# Glossary (full)

🟢 **Reference.** The exhaustive glossary of every term used in the BoxedUp guide and Discord. Bookmark and reference whenever a term comes up you don't recognize.

## A

**A2A** — Amazon-to-Amazon arbitrage. Buying from Amazon at a temporarily mispriced price, then reselling on Amazon when price reverts. See [A2A page](../sourcing/a2a.md).

**A+ content** — Enhanced product description with images and modules. Available to brand-registered sellers.

**A-to-Z claim** — Customer-filed claim against a seller for issues like late delivery or item-not-as-described. Counts as ODR defect.

**ACOS** — Advertising Cost of Sale. Ad spend ÷ ad-attributed sales. Used in PPC management.

**Account Health** — Seller Central's dashboard showing performance metrics + IP complaints + listing issues.

**Account Health Rating (AHR)** — 0–1000 score summarizing account standing. Below 200 = critical risk.

**ASIN** — Amazon Standard Identification Number. 10-character product ID, starts with `B0`.

**Authorized reseller** — A reseller with documented permission from the brand to sell their products.

**Aged inventory surcharge** — Additional storage fee for inventory aged 271+ days at FBA.

## B

**Backstock** — Inventory not yet on shelves at retail (sometimes accessible to resellers via "shelf-stockers" or specific store programs).

**Bestseller Rank (BSR)** — Sales rank within a category. Lower = sells faster. Often inversely correlated with sales velocity.

**Brand gating** — Brand-level restriction requiring approval to sell. Different from category gating.

**Brand Registry** — Amazon program for trademark holders. Provides listing control + IP enforcement tools.

**BBP / BuyBotPro** — Sourcing scanner with auto-evaluate features.

**Bulky** — FBA size tier for items beyond standard but not extra-large.

**Bundle** — Multi-component product sold as one unit. Has specific FBA listing rules.

**Buy Box** — The featured offer on a listing. Wins ~85% of sales.

**Buy Box %** — Percentage of time you win Buy Box for a given listing.

## C

**Cannibalization** — When one of your listings reduces sales of another (e.g., similar variations).

**Cash flow** — Money in vs out, not the same as profit. Resellers can be profitable but cash-strapped.

**Cashback** — Reward back from a portal (Rakuten, TopCashback) for online purchases.

**Catalog** — Amazon's complete database of ASINs. New listings get added to it.

**Category gating** — Category-level restriction (Beauty, Grocery, etc.) requiring approval.

**Cease and desist** — Legal demand from brand owner to stop selling. Often precedes IP claims.

**CC** — BoxedUp Command Center, in-house sourcing tool.

**COGS** — Cost of Goods Sold. The cost of inventory you sold.

**Commingled inventory** — Stickerless inventory pooled by UPC across all sellers. Risky.

**Counterfeit** — Fake product. Selling counterfeit (knowingly or not) = severe account action.

**CSDR** — Customer Service Dissatisfaction Rate. % of messages flagged as unhelpful.

**Customer return** — Inventory returned by buyer. Amazon grades + restocks (or destroys).

## D

**DDR4 / DDR5** — RAM specifications. Subject of a notable shortage / restock case study.

**Detail Page Sales and Traffic** — Amazon's report on per-ASIN sales metrics.

**Disposal** — Amazon destroys inventory. Cheaper than removal.

**Distribution** — Wholesale chain through which products reach retail.

**Drop-shipping** — Shipping inventory directly from supplier to customer without inspecting yourself. Risky on Amazon.

## E

**EIN** — Employer Identification Number. Federal tax ID for businesses.

**Entity** — Legal business structure (LLC, sole prop, S-corp, etc.).

## F

**FA / FlipAlert** — Third-party deal-alert service.

**FBA** — Fulfilled by Amazon. Amazon stores, packs, ships your inventory.

**FBM** — Fulfilled by Merchant. You ship from your own location.

**Fee preview** — Amazon's tool showing estimated FBA fees per ASIN.

**FIFO** — First In, First Out. Inventory accounting method default for resellers.

**FNSKU** — Fulfillment Network SKU. Amazon's barcode for your specific listing.

**Found Profit List** — Spreadsheet tracking every profitable item you've identified.

## G

**Gated** — Brand or category requiring approval to sell.

**Gating** — The process of restricting sales to approved sellers.

**Generic / private label** — A product without an established brand, often manufactured for resale.

**Grade** — Used inventory condition (Like New, Very Good, Good, Acceptable).

**Gray market** — Products sold through unofficial distribution. Brand may complain even though product is authentic.

## H

**Hazmat** — Hazardous Materials. Includes batteries, aerosols, flammables. Requires special FBA approval.

**Hijacker** — Unauthorized seller listing on a Brand Registered listing. Brand can file IP complaint.

**Honey** — Browser extension for finding coupon codes.

## I

**IL / Inventory Lab** — Sourcing/listing/COGS tracking software.

**In-stock rate** — % of time top SKUs are in stock. Affects IPI.

**Inauthentic complaint** — Customer or brand claim that an item is not authentic. Triggers Account Health action.

**Inbound shipping** — Shipping from your location to Amazon's FBA warehouse.

**Inbound placement fee** — Amazon's fee for shipment placement options.

**Inventory Performance Index (IPI)** — Amazon's score of how well you manage FBA inventory. 0–1000.

**IP complaint** — Intellectual Property complaint filed against a seller.

## K

**Keepa** — Price + rank tracking tool. Essential for sourcing.

**Keepa Deals** — Real-time stream of items on sale. Used for A2A.

**KPF** — Keepa Product Finder. Query Amazon's catalog with custom filters.

## L

**Landed cost** — Total cost per unit including buy price, shipping, prep, etc.

**Large standard** — FBA size tier between small and oversize.

**LSR** — Late Shipment Rate. FBM defect metric.

**Liquidation** — Reseller buys pallets of returns/overstock at deep discount.

**Listing** — Amazon's product page. Each ASIN has one listing.

**LLC** — Limited Liability Company. Common business entity for resellers.

**Logo** — Some sellers use a custom logo for branding their bundles.

**LTSF** — Long-Term Storage Fee. Penalty on inventory 365+ days old.

## M

**MAP** — Minimum Advertised Price. Brand's lowest authorized price.

**MOQ** — Minimum Order Quantity. Wholesale minimum.

**MPF** — Marketplace Facilitator. Laws requiring marketplaces (Amazon) to collect sales tax.

**MSDS** — Material Safety Data Sheet. Required for hazmat items.

**MSRP** — Manufacturer's Suggested Retail Price.

**Multi-pack** — Multiple of same product sold as one unit. Specific listing rules apply.

## N

**Net profit** — Revenue minus all costs (fees, COGS, expenses).

**Nexus** — Tax-presence in a state. Amazon's MPF reduced multi-state nexus issues.

## O

**OA** — Online Arbitrage. Sourcing from online retailers.

**Offer count** — Total number of sellers (FBA + FBM) currently on a listing.

**ODR** — Order Defect Rate. % of orders with defects (negative feedback, A-to-Z, chargeback).

**Online retailer** — Walmart.com, Target.com, etc. Source for OA.

**OOS** — Out of Stock.

**Optimized shipment splits** — Amazon's recommended shipment placement option.

**Overstock** — Excess inventory at retail or distributor level.

## P

**Pallet** — Bulk shipment of returned/overstock inventory. Used in liquidation.

**Pile-on** — When too many sellers source the same deal, tanking the listing price.

**PL** — Private Label. Selling your own brand of product.

**PPC** — Pay-Per-Click advertising. Sponsored Product / Sponsored Brand campaigns.

**Pre-fulfillment cancel rate** — % of orders cancelled before shipping.

**Prep** — Preparing inventory for FBA: polybagging, labeling, etc.

**Prep service** — Third-party that handles prep + shipping for fee.

**Profit margin** — Profit ÷ Revenue.

## Q

**Q4** — October–December. Highest-volume period for FBA. Special rules apply.

**QR code** — 2D barcode used in Amazon's newer 2D barcode workflow.

## R

**RA** — Retail Arbitrage. Sourcing from physical stores.

**Rakuten** — Cashback portal. 1–10% back on purchases.

**Receive time** — Time between Amazon receiving your shipment and inventory becoming sellable.

**Referral fee** — Amazon's commission on each sale (% of sale price, varies by category).

**Reimbursement** — Amazon's payment to you for lost/damaged inventory.

**Removal order** — Sending inventory back to you from FBA.

**Replen / Replenishable** — A product you can re-source from same channel multiple times.

**Reseller certificate** — State-issued certificate allowing tax-free purchase of inventory for resale.

**Restock limit** — Cap on inventory volume at FBA, tied to IPI.

**Restock/Monitor** — Sourcing strategy: identify OOS items, monitor for restocks. See [Restock strategy](../sourcing/restock-monitor-strategy.md).

**Returns processing fee** — Fee charged on returns in some categories.

**Returns reserve** — Money set aside for expected returns (~5% of revenue).

**ROI** — Return on Investment. Profit ÷ Cost. Common arb threshold: 30%+.

## S

**S-corp** — Tax election for LLC. Saves SE tax above ~\$60K profit.

**SAS** — SellerAmp SAS. Sourcing scanner.

**SB / SellerBoard** — P&L + reimbursement automation tool.

**SE tax** — Self-employment tax. 15.3% on sole prop / single-member LLC profit.

**Search term** — Keywords customers use to find products.

**Seasonal** — Product with seasonal demand pattern.

**Seller Central** — Amazon's seller dashboard.

**Sellerise** — Multi-account analytics + reinstatement help service.

**SFP** — Seller Fulfilled Prime. Premium FBM tier with Prime SLA.

**Shrinkage** — Inventory loss (theft, damage, mis-counts).

**SKU** — Your internal product identifier.

**Sliver of orange** — Tiny brief restock event in Keepa. High-conviction demand signal.

**Small standard** — Smallest FBA size tier.

**Sponsored Product** — Amazon's pay-per-click ad type.

**Stickerless** — Inventory commingled by UPC instead of FNSKU. Risky.

**Storefront** — A seller's collection of all their listings. Brand-registered storefronts have custom branding.

**Stranded inventory** — Inventory at FBA that can't be sold (suppressed listing, missing details).

**Streamlined Sales Tax (SST)** — Multi-state tax-cooperation program.

**Subcategory rank** — More granular than top-level BSR. Often more useful.

**Suspension** — Account-level enforcement action by Amazon.

## T

**Tank** — When a listing's price drops sharply.

**Tariff** — Import tax. Affects PL sourcing.

**Tempo** — Restock monitor bot. Common in BoxedUp Discord.

**Threshold** — Minimum criteria for a deal (ROI floor, BSR ceiling, etc.).

**Tier 1 brand** — High-IP-enforcement brands. Examples: Disney, Nintendo, Apple.

**Tier 2 brand** — Moderate-enforcement brands. Examples: Sony, Bose.

**Tier 3 brand** — Permissive-enforcement brands. Most generics.

**Trademark** — Registered brand mark. Required for Brand Registry.

**Transparency Program** — Anti-counterfeit per-unit codes.

**Trial** — A new account's first 90-day probationary period.

## U

**UPC** — Universal Product Code. Barcode on retail products.

**Use tax** — Tax owed on tax-free purchases that get consumed (not resold).

## V

**Variation** — Different versions of a product (sizes, colors) on the same listing.

**Vendor Central** — B2B program where you sell to Amazon directly. Different from Seller Central.

**Veritiv** — Bulk supply company for polybags + packaging.

**Vine Voices** — Amazon's invited-reviewer program. Available to brand-registered sellers.

**Volume** — Number of units sold over time.

## W

**WISMO** — "Where Is My Order?" Common customer question type.

**Wholesale** — Direct sourcing from brand or distributor.

**Wholesale invoice** — Document proving authorized wholesale purchase. Strong defense for IP claims.

## Y

**Year-end** — Tax / accounting close-out.

## Z

**Z-code** — Special FBA box label format (legacy term, less common now).
