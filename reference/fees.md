# Fee reference (FBA, referral, storage)

🟢 **Reference.** Quick-lookup tables for Amazon's most common fees. Always verify with [Amazon's FBA Revenue Calculator](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/revcal) for current exact numbers — fees change.

> ⚠️ **Last updated 2026-05-05.** Amazon updates fees at least annually. Confirm current rates before relying for sourcing decisions.

## FBA fees by size tier

Amazon's pick-pack fee. Charged per unit shipped.

### Small Standard
**Dimensions:** ≤15" × 12" × 0.75", weight ≤16oz

| Weight | Fee (2024) |
|---|---|
| ≤4 oz | \$3.06 |
| 4 oz – 8 oz | \$3.15 |
| 8 oz – 12 oz | \$3.24 |
| 12 oz – 16 oz | \$3.43 |

### Large Standard
**Dimensions:** ≤18" × 14" × 8", weight ≤20 lbs

| Weight | Fee (2024) |
|---|---|
| ≤4 oz | \$3.43 |
| 4 oz – 8 oz | \$3.54 |
| 8 oz – 12 oz | \$3.62 |
| 12 oz – 16 oz | \$3.86 |
| 1 – 1.5 lbs | \$4.30 |
| 1.5 – 2 lbs | \$4.84 |
| 2 – 3 lbs | \$5.23 |
| 3 – 20 lbs | \$5.23 + \$0.16/lb above 3 lbs |

**Dimensions:** ≤59" × 33" × 33", weight ≤50 lbs

| Weight | Fee (2024) |
|---|---|
| ≤50 lbs | \$8.30 + \$0.38/lb above 1 lb |

### Extra-Large 0–50 lbs
**Dimensions:** longest side ≤59", weight ≤50 lbs

| Weight | Fee (2024) |
|---|---|
| ≤50 lbs | \$24.59 + \$0.38/lb above 1 lb |

### Extra-Large 50–70 lbs

\$31.91 + \$0.38/lb above 51 lbs

### Extra-Large 70–150 lbs

\$71.10 + \$0.38/lb above 71 lbs

> **Practical takeaway:** small items pay ~\$3.20, medium ~\$5, oversize ~\$10+, extra-oversize \$25+. Plan margins accordingly.

## Storage fees

Charged monthly on the 7th–15th of the following month.

### Standard size (off-peak: Jan–Sep)
**\$0.87 per cubic foot per month**

For a typical small standard item (0.014 cuft):
- Per unit per month: ~\$0.012

### Standard size (peak: Oct–Dec)
**\$2.40 per cubic foot per month**

For same item:
- Per unit per month: ~\$0.034

### Oversize (off-peak)
**\$0.56 per cubic foot per month**

### Oversize (peak)
**\$1.40 per cubic foot per month**

> **Practical math:** 1,000 small standard units stored for 12 months = ~\$144 in base storage. Add seasonal surcharges and aged inventory penalties for full picture.

## Aged inventory surcharge

Added to monthly storage when units age past thresholds:

| Age | Standard (per cuft/month) | Oversize (per cuft/month) |
|---|---|---|
| 271–365 days | +\$1.50 | +\$1.00 |
| 365+ days | +\$6.90 | +\$3.50 |

For typical small std (0.014 cuft) at 365+ days:
- Storage + surcharge: ~\$0.108/month per unit
- Compounds quickly across 100s of units

## Long-Term Storage Fee (LTSF)

Charged twice yearly (Aug 15, Feb 15) on units 365+ days old:

**\$6.90 per cubic foot OR \$0.15 per unit, whichever is greater**

Per-unit minimum dominates for small items: **\$0.15 minimum per unit per assessment.** Two assessments per year = \$0.30/year minimum on aged inventory.

For a unit you bought at \$5 cost, LTSF can eat 6%+ per year. Plus storage. Plus aged surcharge.

**Don't let inventory age past 270 days.** Drop price aggressively or remove/dispose.

## Inbound placement fee

Newer fee charged based on shipment splits:

| Placement option | Fee per unit |
|---|---|
| Amazon-Optimized Shipment Splits | \$0.00–\$0.30 |
| Partial Shipment Splits | \$0.50–\$1.00 |
| Minimal Shipment Splits | \$1.00–\$1.50 |

For a 50-unit shipment: \$0–\$15 for optimized vs \$50–\$75 for minimal. **Always pick optimized.**

## Returns processing fee

Applies to apparel + footwear in some categories:

- ~\$0.50–\$1.00 per return processed
- Charged regardless of refund issued
- Built into the cost of sourcing in those categories

## Removal / disposal fees

When you take inventory out of FBA:

| Action | Standard | Oversize |
|---|---|---|
| Removal (back to you) | \$0.97 | \$3.50–\$6.50 |
| Disposal (Amazon destroys) | \$0.30–\$0.70 | \$1.50–\$3.00 |
| Liquidation | Free + Amazon takes 85%+ | Same |

**Disposal is fastest IPI-rescue tool.** Don't be sentimental about \$5 items.

## Reimbursement processing

Amazon's reimbursement payments:
- For lost units: based on average sale price OR replacement cost
- For damaged: similar
- For weight overcharges: actual fee difference + sometimes interest

You can claim reimbursement up to **18 months from the event.** Older claims forfeit.

## Stranded inventory penalty

If your inventory is stranded (listing suppressed) for >30 days:
- ~\$3.40/cuft surcharge added to base storage
- Drops to standard once listing is fixed

**Always audit stranded inventory weekly.** Fix or remove.

## "Per unit" math for typical SKU

A worked example — \$25 sale, small std unit, 1 lb:

```
Sale price                          $25.00
- Referral fee (15%)                -$3.75
- FBA pick/pack (small std, 1 lb)   -$3.86
- Inbound shipping (~$0.40/unit)    -$0.40
- Inbound placement (optimized)     -$0.20
- Storage (1 month, off-peak)       -$0.012
- Returns reserve (5%)              -$1.25
─────
Total Amazon-related costs:         -$9.47
Net to seller:                      $15.53
```

Subtract your COGS to get net profit. For a \$8 cost, net profit per unit = \$7.53.

## Fee changes to watch for

Amazon updates fees regularly:
- **Annual updates** — usually January
- **Q4 surge fees** — October surge for Christmas
- **New fee categories** — like inbound placement (introduced 2024)

Subscribe to Amazon's "Selling Partner Updates" email. Major fee changes are announced 30–60 days in advance.

## When fees seem wrong

If your actual reported fees differ from estimated:

### Common cause: wrong dimensions/weight
Amazon's measurement may differ from yours. Real-world units sometimes get "remeasured" by Amazon.

**Fix:** check Inventory → Fee Preview → individual ASIN. Compare to your numbers. If Amazon's measurements are wrong, file a fee correction request.

### Common cause: wrong category
Amazon assigned a different category than expected.

**Fix:** verify ASIN's actual category in Inventory → Manage Inventory.

### Common cause: hazmat reclassification
Amazon flagged a unit as hazmat after the fact. Higher fees apply.

**Fix:** for non-hazmat items wrongly flagged, file appeal.
