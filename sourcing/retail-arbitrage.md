# RA — Retail Arbitrage

🟢 → 🟡 — the on-ramp for nearly every Amazon reseller. You walk into a store, scan products, buy mispriced inventory, and ship it to FBA.

## Why RA is the best place to start

- **Cheapest education.** You learn what gates exist, how Keepa charts behave, and what FBA prep feels like — for the cost of a few \$30 mistakes.
- **No supplier relationships needed.** Walmart doesn't care that you're flipping their clearance.
- **Cash converts fast.** Buy Sunday → ship Monday → live on Amazon Friday → sold within 2 weeks if you sourced right.
- **It scales until it doesn't.** Most BoxedUp sellers move to OA + wholesale around \$5–10K/mo because driving stops being worth it.

## The 90-second store-floor workflow

This is what an actual scan looks like once you've got reps:

1. **See yellow / red sticker** (clearance) → take 2 steps closer
2. **Pull phone**, open SellerAmp SAS (or BoxedUp CC's Manual Scan)
3. **Scan the UPC** with the camera
4. SAS shows: sell price, BSR, ROI, fees, FBA seller count, gating status
5. **Decision tree:**
   - 🟢 **Green light** (ROI ≥ 30%, BSR top 1%, ungated, ≤3 FBA sellers) → check shelf for quantity, fill cart
   - 🟡 **Yellow** (one weak signal) → check Keepa chart manually, take a photo, decide
   - 🔴 **Red** (gated / hijackers / IP brand / tanking price) → keep walking
6. Repeat for ~90 minutes per store visit

> **Pro tip:** the first 30 days, scan *everything* — even items you "know" won't be profitable. You're training your gut on what triggers the green light. After 90 days, your eyes will pre-filter and you'll only scan ~10% of what you used to.

## What to scan (and what to skip)

**Always scan:**
- Anything with a clearance sticker
- End caps (front-of-aisle displays — usually moved-to-clear)
- Seasonal aisles 30+ days after the season (Halloween in November, Christmas in January)
- Toys with damaged packaging at heavy discount
- LEGO sets (every set is a known flip — use BoxedUp CC's saved-search for this)

**Skip:**
- Anything Amazon sells directly on the listing (you'll never win the Buy Box)
- Brands you know are gated (Nike, Disney, Nintendo, LEGO without ungating, beauty majors)
- Anything under \$3 sell price (FBA fees eat it)
- Glass / liquid / pressurized cans (HazMat, prep nightmare)

## The unspoken rules

⚠️ **Don't be a jerk in stores.** This is the #1 way RA gets killed for everyone:

- Don't strip shelves clean — leave some for the next person
- Don't argue with employees about price-matching online
- Don't pull product from back-stock — what's on the shelf is what's available
- Don't scan in obvious places where employees are watching
- Don't return inventory you couldn't sell — that's fraud, and stores track it

## Receipts are everything

Save **every receipt**. Your future self will thank you when:

- A brand owner files an inauthentic claim and you need to prove sourcing
- It's tax time and you need COGS records
- Amazon asks for invoices to ungate a category (RA receipts often don't qualify, but for some unbranded items they do)

> **Best practice:** photo every receipt with your phone, store in a Google Drive folder by date, log into BoxedUp CC's Leads tab or a spreadsheet. Receipts fade — phone photos don't.

## RA + replenishables = the real game

The myth is "RA is one-and-done finds." The reality: ~30% of profitable RA finds are **replens** — products you can rebuy from the same store at the same price for months.

Build a **replen list** in BoxedUp CC's Leads tab from day one. Every Sunday, check which replens are still in stock at your stores via:

- Walmart app (shelf availability per store)
- BrickSeek (Walmart, Target, Lowes inventory checker)
- BoxedUp CC's Walmart In-Store tab

Reorder the ones still in stock. Toss the dead leads. Over 6 months, your replen list compounds and your scan time per profitable unit drops 5–10×.

## The 30-day starter routine

Week 1–2: visit 3 stores per week, scan 90 min each. Goal: **find 5 profitable items.**

Week 3–4: ship those 5 items into FBA. Goal: **finish your first shipping plan without panicking.**

After that:
- Add one new store type per week
- Build a replen list in BoxedUp CC
- Graduate to OA after ~60 RA flips (or sooner if your back hurts)
