# Glossary

The 60 terms you'll hear daily in the BoxedUp Discord. Bookmark this — you'll come back.

> See the [Full glossary](../reference/glossary-full.md) in the Reference section for exhaustive definitions.

## Core Amazon terms

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **ASIN** | Amazon Standard Identification Number — the 10-char product ID (starts with `B0`). |
| **SKU** | Your internal product identifier on Amazon. You set it. |
| **FNSKU** | Amazon's barcode they put on your inventory after FBA prep. |
| **UPC / EAN** | Universal/European product barcodes — what's on the box at retail. |
| **Buy Box** | The featured offer on a listing. Whoever wins it gets ~85%+ of sales. |
| **Listing** | The product page on Amazon (`amazon.com/dp/<ASIN>`). |
| **BSR** | Best Sellers Rank — how a product ranks in its category. Lower = sells faster. |
| **Sales velocity** | Estimated units sold per month. |

## Sourcing terms

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **RA** | Retail Arbitrage — sourcing from physical stores (Walmart, Target, TJ Maxx). |
| **OA** | Online Arbitrage — sourcing from online retailers. |
| **WS** | Wholesale — buying directly from a brand or distributor with a reseller cert. |
| **Liq** | Liquidation — pallets of returned/overstock inventory from B-Stock, BULQ, etc. |
| **PL** | Private Label — selling your own brand. (Not what most BoxedUp folks do.) |
| **Lead** | A profitable product/source pairing — usually shared in deal channels. |
| **Replen** | A product you can buy and resell repeatedly. |
| **OOS** | Out of stock. |
| **Tank** | Price drops sharply on Amazon. "Don't buy that — it's gonna tank." |

## FBA mechanics

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **FBA** | Fulfilled By Amazon — they store, pack, ship for you. |
| **FBM** | Fulfilled By Merchant — you ship from your home/warehouse. |
| **SFP** | Seller Fulfilled Prime — Prime badge but you ship. (Hard to qualify.) |
| **IPI** | Inventory Performance Index — Amazon's "are you a good FBA citizen" score (0–1000). |
| **Shipment** | A box/pallet you send to an FBA warehouse. |
| **Prep** | Polybagging, bubble wrapping, labeling before shipment. |
| **Removal order** | Asking Amazon to ship your inventory back to you (or destroy it). |
| **Restock limits** | Caps on how much inventory FBA will accept. Tied to IPI. |

## Tools & community

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Keepa** | The chart-and-data product tracker every reseller uses. |
| **SAS** | SellerAmp SAS — the most-used deal-analysis scanner. |
| **IL** | Inventory Lab — bookkeeping + listing software. |
| **SB** | SellerBoard — profit-tracking dashboard + reimbursement automation. |
| **KPF** | Keepa Product Finder — query the Keepa database for products matching filters. |

## Account health

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **AH** | Account Health page in Seller Central. |
| **ODR** | Order Defect Rate — must stay under 1%. |
| **LSR** | Late Shipment Rate (FBM only). |
| **POA** | Plan of Action — what you submit when appealing a suspension. |
| **IP claim** | Intellectual property complaint from a brand owner. |
| **Inauthentic** | A complaint that your item is fake (often falsely). |
| **Gated** | A brand or category you need approval to sell in. |
| **Hijacker** | Someone listing on your private-label listing without permission. |

## Fee math

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Referral fee** | Amazon's cut of the sale price — usually 8–15%, most often **15%**. |
| **FBA fee** | The pick/pack fee — varies by size & weight. |
| **Storage fee** | Monthly cost to store inventory at FBA. Higher Q4. |
| **LTSF** | Long-Term Storage Fee — penalties for inventory aged 271+ days. |
| **Inbound placement fee** | Newer fee charged for sending shipments to fewer warehouses. |
| **ROI** | Return on Investment = profit / cost. |
| **Margin** | Profit / sell price. |
| **COGS** | Cost of Goods Sold — what you paid for the inventory. |
| **Net** | Profit after all fees. |

## Discord shorthand

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Bind** | The buy/cost link in a deal post. |
| **Sell** | The Amazon listing link in a deal post. |
| **Threshold** | A profit/ROI rule that auto-fires deals into a channel. |
| **Drop** | A new lead posted in a deal channel. |
| **Cooked** | A deal that's been over-shared — too many sellers, price will tank. |
| **Tanked** | Price already dropped, deal is dead. |
