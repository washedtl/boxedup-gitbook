# Pro vs Individual: which plan

🟢 **Beginner** — read this before you click "register."

The first decision Amazon makes you make, and most beginners get it wrong.

## TL;DR

> **If you're serious, get Professional from day one. Period.**

The \$39.99/month "Pro" plan pays for itself at **40 sales/month** — and you literally cannot run an FBA business at scale on Individual.

## The two plans

| Feature | **Individual** | **Professional** |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly fee | \$0 | \$39.99 |
| Per-item fee | \$0.99 / sale | None |
| Buy Box eligible | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |
| Bulk listing tools | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |
| Add new ASINs to catalog | Limited | ✅ Yes |
| Restricted categories | ❌ No | ✅ Apply for ungating |
| API access (for tools like SAS, IL) | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |
| Sponsored ads | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |
| Reports (FBA Inventory, Reimbursements) | Limited | ✅ Full |

## Why "Individual" looks tempting (and is a trap)

Beginners look at the \$39.99/mo and think "I'll save money until I'm selling regularly." Three problems with that:

1. **No Buy Box** = your listings exist but nobody sees them. You'll get one sale a week.
2. **No API access** = SellerAmp, InventoryLab, and other reseller tools stop working.
3. **The \$0.99 per sale crosses over fast.** 40 sales = \$39.60 in per-item fees. You're already paying for Pro and getting none of the benefits.

> **The math:** if you do even 2 flips a week (≈8/mo), upgrading to Pro saves you \$0.99 × 8 = \$7.92 in per-item fees against \$32 of net cost (\$39.99 − \$7.92), and unlocks Buy Box + ungating + API. It's not close.

## When Individual makes sense

Honestly? Almost never for an aspiring FBA seller. Two narrow cases:

- You're **selling a single item** (used books, one-off items from your closet) and you'll never scale.
- You want to **test the account creation flow** before committing — Amazon lets you upgrade later with one click.

Even then, the rule is: **upgrade the moment you decide to source seriously.**

## How to upgrade (or downgrade)

1. Seller Central → **Settings** (top right) → **Account Info**
2. Under "Your Services" → **Manage** → toggle to Professional
3. Confirm. Takes effect immediately. Next month's \$39.99 hits the card on file.

⚠️ **Heads up:** Amazon prorates Pro for the current month, but you don't get a refund of \$0.99 fees already paid. Switch at the start of the month if possible.

## What you don't get on either plan

A few myths to clear up:

- ❌ Neither plan auto-enrolls you in Brand Registry. That's separate. (See [Brand Registry](brand-registry.md).)
- ❌ Neither plan auto-ungates restricted categories like Grocery, Beauty, or Topicals. You apply, plan-by-plan.
- ❌ Neither plan exempts you from sales tax. Marketplace Facilitator handles most of it, but you still need a state-level setup. (See [Sales tax](../business-setup/sales-tax.md).)

## The BoxedUp recommendation

| You are… | Plan |
|---|---|
| Just curious, browsing | Don't open an account yet. Read more of this guide first. |
| Going to make 5+ flips this month | **Professional** — day one |
| Already selling on Individual | Upgrade today. You're leaving money on the table every sale. |
| Selling once a year on used books | Individual is fine |
